VPAINT QA Terminal App

To guarantee transparency in the B2B manufacturing supply chain, VPAINT EOOD provides a dedicated mobile tracking application.

The QA Terminal allows our industrial clients to scan QR codes on delivered pallets to instantly access digital quality assurance reports, coating thickness specs, and heat treatment logs from their Android devices.

  • Real-time tracking: "In Furnace", "Coating", "Curing".
  • In-app Barcode/QR Scanner for instant batch verification.
  • Digital ISO compliance and Quality Assurance certificates.
  • Direct messaging with VPAINT metallurgical engineers.
